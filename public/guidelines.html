‚úÖ What's Welcome Here

Encouraged Content:

  • Genuine missed connections (you saw someone in public and wish you'd said hi)
  • Respectful, specific descriptions ("You had short blue hair and were reading...")
  • General location info (neighborhood, venue type - NOT exact addresses)
  • Posts about wanting to reconnect with someone you briefly met
  • Hopeful, kind, and sincere messages

Good Posting Practices:

  • Be specific enough to be recognizable, but not creepy
  • Include context about where/when you saw them
  • Express what you wish you'd said or done
  • Be respectful of boundaries
  • Keep it PG-13 (this isn't a hookup app)

üö´ What's Not Allowed

Prohibited Content:

  1. Men posting or seeking connections with women - This is a sapphic-only space
  2. Sexual or explicit content - No soliciting nudes, hookups, or explicit encounters
  3. Harassment or stalking behavior - No repeated posts about the same person or revealing private info
  4. Hate speech or discrimination - No transphobia, biphobia, racism, ableism, or any form of bigotry. TERF rhetoric will result in immediate ban.
  5. Spam or advertising - No promoting businesses, MLM schemes, or soliciting money
  6. Scams or fraud - No asking for money, gift cards, or financial help
  7. Minors - You must be 18+ to use this platform
  8. Illegal content - No posts about illegal activities or drug dealing
