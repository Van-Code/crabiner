ğŸ§  How Crabiner works (and why this matters)

Crabiner is recognition-based, not discovery-based.

  • You post a moment that already happened.
  • You do not post identifying information.
  • Replies should only be possible if the other person was truly there.

If someone was not present for the moment, they should not be able to convincingly respond.

This structure is part of the safety.

Encouraged posts

  • Genuine missed moments from real-world interactions
  • Contextual details the right person would recognize
  • General locations (neighborhood, venue type, event)
  • Kind, sincere, PG-13 tone

Good Posting Practices:

  • Be specific, but not invasive
  • Focus on what happened, not who the person is
  • Share what you wish you had said or done
  • Accept that not every post will get a response
