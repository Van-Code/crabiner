## ‚úÖ What's Welcome Here

### **Encouraged Content:**

- Genuine missed connections (you saw someone in public and wish you'd said hi)
- Respectful, specific descriptions ("You had short blue hair and were reading...")
- General location info (neighborhood, venue type - NOT exact addresses)
- Posts about wanting to reconnect with someone you briefly met
- Hopeful, kind, and sincere messages

### **Good Posting Practices:**

- Be specific enough to be recognizable, but not creepy
- Include context about where/when you saw them
- Express what you wish you'd said or done
- Be respectful of boundaries
- Keep it PG-13 (this isn't a hookup app)

## üö´ What's Not Allowed

### **Prohibited Content:**

1. **Men posting or seeking connections with women**

   - This is a sapphic-only space

2. **Sexual or explicit content**

   - No soliciting nudes, hookups, or explicit encounters
   - Keep descriptions respectful and appropriate
   - This isn't a dating app - it's for missed connections

3. **Harassment or stalking behavior**

   - No repeated posts about the same person
   - No posts that reveal someone's private info (workplace, home, etc.)
   - No posts that make someone feel unsafe or followed

4. **Hate speech or discrimination**

   - No transphobia, biphobia, racism, ableism, or any form of bigotry
   - Trans women are women - TERF rhetoric will result in immediate ban
   - Respect all identities within the sapphic community

5. **Spam or advertising**

   - No promoting businesses, services, or products
   - No MLM schemes or financial solicitations
   - No soliciting money or "sugar daddy/mama" arrangements

6. **Scams or fraud**

   - No asking for money, gift cards, or financial help
   - No phishing attempts or suspicious links
   - No catfishing or impersonation

7. **Minors**

   - You must be 18+ to use this platform
   - No posts about or seeking contact with minors
   - No posts that mention high school or underage settings

8. **Illegal content**
   - No posts about illegal activities
   - No drug dealing or solicitation
   - No posts that could facilitate harm

## ü¶î How to Use This Space Safely

### **For Posters:**

1. **Protect your privacy**

   - Don't share your last name, workplace, or home address
   - Use general locations ("Castro neighborhood" not "123 Main St")
   - Save your inbox link securely

2. **Be specific but not creepy**

   - ‚úÖ "You were at Blue Bottle on Monday morning wearing a Pride pin"
   - ‚ùå "I've been watching you at this coffee shop for 3 weeks"

3. **Set realistic expectations**

   - Not everyone will see your post
   - The person may not remember you
   - They may not be interested - and that's okay

4. **Trust your instincts**
   - If a reply feels off, don't respond
   - You can delete messages or your whole post anytime
   - Block and report concerning behavior

### **For Repliers:**

1. **Be respectful**

   - Even if you don't remember them, be kind
   - It takes courage to post - don't mock or shame

2. **Verify it's really you**

   - Include details only the person who saw you would know
   - Be honest if you're not sure it's about you

3. **Respect "no"**

   - If they don't respond, don't push
   - One message is enough

4. **Keep initial messages appropriate**
   - Introduce yourself
   - Reference the situation they described
   - Suggest a low-pressure way to connect (coffee, public place)

### **How to Report:**

1. Click the "üö© Report Post" button on any post

- Content flagged by our safety filter is reviewed immediately
- Serious violations may result in IP bans

## üíï Consent & Boundaries
